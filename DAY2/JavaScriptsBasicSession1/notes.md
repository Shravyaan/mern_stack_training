  - Inline

1. Variables in Js
   - var (can be updated and re-declared)

   ````var x = 10
   var x = 20```

   - let (this can be updated but no re-declaration)
   ``` let x = 10
       let x = 20 (Invalid)```

   - const (no updation and no re-declaration)
   ````
